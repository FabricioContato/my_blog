What is this blog ?

My Journey

Here, I document my journey as a web developer, challenging myself to build new features for this blog. No CMS — I'm starting from scratch and building everything one step at a time. You can expect a mix of things a blog should (and maybe shouldn't) have:

  • Comment section
  • Pagination
  • Mini Games
  • Code Sandbox
  • ETC...
About Me

I'm an IT freelancer who enjoys programming and continuous learning. I want to work and grow, and this blog is a blend of both. It's not just about personal branding—it's also a way for me to develop new skills and embrace new challenges.

I'm a big fan of sharing knowledge. I’ve always appreciated finding great learning resources online, and now that I’m the one writing, I’ve discovered how much I enjoy documenting and sharing my journey as a developer. I aim to do it with quality and intention.

At the time of writing, I’m just getting started with blogging, so I’m still figuring things out.
